Welcome to Siri Academy

Siri Academy is among the foremost educational institutes, dedicated to empowering numerous schools and colleges across India. We offer a comprehensive range of educational services, spanning from pre-school to pre-university programs, including coaching classes for competitive exams such as NEET, JEE, and CET.

Train-Testify

Our philosophy, "train-testify," ensures that parents are fully informed about their child's performance. Understanding the importance of technology in modern education, Siri Academy integrates advanced methods to enhance learning experiences.

We are eager to establish a strategic association with the best institutions to promote the welfare and academic success of both students and management.

Our Story

Siri Academy operates on a corporate model, collaborating with renowned educational institutions to help students achieve significant career milestones through a well-crafted curriculum and highly efficient faculty. We own or have access to various proprietary materials, and possess the expertise in faculty selection, teaching schedule coordination, educational standard administration, and examination procedures.

  • Legacy of decades
  • Secured Top ranks in competitive exams
  • Trained over 5,000 aspiring medical and engineering students

Conceptualized by Mr. Rao, aims to transform the lives of thousands of aspirants into Scientists, Doctors, and Engineers. Our premier organization develops critical thinking and provides professional training for high school, secondary, and senior secondary students, equipping them with the skills needed to excel in NTSE, Olympiads, NEET, JEE, KVPY, and other competitive exams.

Our Chairman

K S RAO

Founder & Chairman

Mr. K.V.V. Sambasiva Rao is an esteemed educationist and mathematics teacher with 30 years of experience in the field of academics. Throughout his career, he has held prominent positions as the HOD and as an IIT faculty member (Mathematics) in prestigious organizations in the Telugu-speaking states.

His journey began in Karnataka in 2007 as a mathematics lecturer and later took on the roles of principal for an IIT campus and academic coordinator for all branches in Karnataka. He played a pivotal role in helping numerous aspirants achieve their dreams of becoming IITians and Medicos.

Currently, he is the founder and head of Siri Academy and serves as the CEO of Sunshine Group of Educational Institutions.
